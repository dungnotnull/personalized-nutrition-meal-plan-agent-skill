## Core Concepts & Frameworks
- **DRI/RDA (NIH/USDA):** reference intakes for macros & micronutrients by age/sex.
- **USDA MyPlate / Dietary Guidelines for Americans:** food-group balance.
- **Mediterranean & DASH diets:** strongest evidence for cardiometabolic outcomes.
- **TDEE estimation:** Mifflin-St Jeor (preferred) / Harris-Benedict + activity factor.
- **Energy balance:** ~3,500 kcal ≈ 0.45 kg fat; safe deficit 10–20% of TDEE.
- **Protein targets:** 1.6–2.2 g/kg for muscle gain/retention (ISSN position).
- **Micronutrient adequacy:** iron, calcium, vitamin D, B12, omega-3 commonly under-consumed.

## Safety Red Flags (screen before any plan)
Disordered-eating indicators, BMI <16 or rapid loss, pregnancy/lactation, type 1/2 diabetes, CKD, eating with GI disease, drug-nutrient interactions (e.g., warfarin/vitamin K), severe allergies → refer to RD/physician.
